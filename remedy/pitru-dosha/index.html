🪔 Presiding Deity: Pitru Devatas (Ancestors)

What is Pitru Dosha?

Pitru Dosha occurs due to unfulfilled desires or wrongdoings of ancestors. It manifests as repeated family issues, childlessness, or financial stagnation.

🎵
Primary Mantra / Bhajan for Remedy
🪔
Recommended Daily Practice
Pitru Tarpan
Dedicated to Pitru Devatas (Ancestors) · Recite with sincere devotion
🪴
Step-by-Step Remedial Actions
1
Perform Tarpan on Amavasya (no-moon day)
2
Offer water with black sesame seeds to ancestors daily
3
Feed Brahmins or the poor on Amavasya
4
Perform Shraadh rituals during Pitru Paksha
⚠ Severity Notice
Severity: Moderate to High
Important Guidance for Pitru Dosha

Pitru Dosha can span generations if unaddressed. The good news is that sincere Shraadh rituals and ancestor veneration can resolve it relatively quickly. Perform these rites with pure intention during Pitru Paksha each year.
