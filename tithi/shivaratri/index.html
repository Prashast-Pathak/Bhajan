About Maha Shivaratri

The great night of Shiva. The most powerful night of the year for Shiva worship, occurring on the 14th day of the Krishna Paksha of Phalguna month.

🌿
Fasting Guidance (Upvas Vidhi)
Fast Type
Full Day & Night
Intensity
🔴 Most Intense
Full day and night fast. Puja is performed in 4 prahar (night watches).
🎵
Primary Mantra / Stotram
🔱
Recite on this Tithi
Om Namah Shivaya — chanted 108, 1008, or 10008 times through the night
Offered with devotion to Lord Shiva
