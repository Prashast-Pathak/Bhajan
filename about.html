Our Mission

हमारा उद्देश्य

सनातन ज्ञान सागर is a free, open, and accessible platform dedicated to preserving and sharing the eternal wisdom of Hinduism — for every person, on every device, in every language they are comfortable with.

We believe that sacred knowledge belongs to everyone. Whether you are an 80-year-old grandmother on a cheap Android phone or a 28-year-old IT professional on an iPhone — this library is built for you.

Our Core Principles

  • ♿
    Accessibility First If an 80-year-old grandmother on a cheap Android cannot use it, it has failed. 22px+ default font, large buttons, offline access.
  • 🔍
    Search-First Design Every piece of content is a Google search landing page with unique, descriptive URLs.
  • 📚
    Depth Over Breadth Complete, rich content beats thin pages. Every bhajan has story, meaning, occasion context and verse-by-verse breakdown.
  • 🆓
    Free Forever Zero cost infrastructure. Everything accessible without creating an account. No login walls ever.
  • 🌐
    Language for All Sanskrit original, Hindi translation, English meaning, and Roman transliteration on every item.
  • 🙏
    Spiritual Integrity Every word must be accurate, authentic, and respectful. We do not take liberties with sacred texts.

Who We Serve

Our primary audience is Hindi-speaking Hindus worldwide — in India (Haryana, UP, Rajasthan, MP, Bihar, Delhi NCR) and the Indian diaspora in USA, UK, Canada, UAE, and Australia.

We serve grandmothers seeking bhajan lyrics for morning puja, professionals looking for Gita wisdom on anxiety, homemakers wanting festival-organized content, seekers finding spiritual answers to modern problems, and pandits needing printable temple collections.

Content Integrity

All content is sourced from authentic Hindu scriptures and scholarly traditions. We do not alter, simplify, or reinterpret sacred texts beyond providing explanations in accessible language.

If you find any error in our content, please contact us. Maintaining the integrity of our sacred content is our highest priority.
