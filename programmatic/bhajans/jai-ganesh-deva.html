जय गणेश देवा

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Deity: Ganesha

Significance: In Hindu tradition, Ganesha is always worshipped first before any prayer, ritual, or new beginning — before building a house, starting a business, beginning studies, or undertaking any journey. Chanting Jai Ganesh Deva is believed to clear all obstacles from the path, bestow divine wisdom, and ensure the success of any endeavor. Ganesha represents the union of the human and the divine — his elephant head symbolizes wisdom and large ears mean he listens to all prayers.
