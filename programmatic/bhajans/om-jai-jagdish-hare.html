ॐ जय जगदीश हरे

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Deity: Vishnu

Significance: Om Jai Jagdish Hare is performed as the concluding aarti in virtually every Hindu puja ceremony. It is the prayer that unites all Hindus regardless of which deity they worship, because it addresses the Supreme God behind all forms. The aarti is also notable for the verse where the devotee says 'Everything is yours — what is mine?' — a complete surrender of ego that lies at the heart of bhakti (devotional) spirituality.
