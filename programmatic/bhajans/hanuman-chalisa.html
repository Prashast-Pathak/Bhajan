Programmatic spiritual reference page

Deity: Hanuman

Significance: The Hanuman Chalisa is the most widely recited Hindu prayer in the world, chanted by hundreds of millions of devotees every day. It is scientifically structured — the 40 verses systematically praise Hanuman's form, qualities, feats, and grace. Reciting it is believed to grant courage in times of fear, protection from evil forces, health in times of illness, success in difficult endeavors, and the supreme blessing of Ram's name. The Chalisa is also a condensed biography of Hanuman's life — from his birth to his legendary feats in the Ramayana.
