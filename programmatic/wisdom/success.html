सफलता और उपलब्धि

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: Hindu scripture does not reject worldly success — it redefines it. True success (siddhi) is not merely external achievement but the internal transformation that accompanies right action. The Gita's genius is in showing that the path to lasting success runs through dharma, non-attachment, and excellence of character — not through the ruthless pursuit of results.
