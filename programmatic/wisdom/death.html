मृत्यु और नश्वरता

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: No philosophical tradition has engaged with death as deeply, honestly, and fearlessly as the Sanatana Dharma. The Bhagavad Gita was born on a battlefield, in the face of mass death. Krishna's answer to Arjuna's grief was not denial but a revelation of the immortal nature of the soul — transforming death from an ending into a transition.
