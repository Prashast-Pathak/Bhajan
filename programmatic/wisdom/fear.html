भय और साहस

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: The Bhagavad Gita opens with Arjuna paralyzed by fear — his limbs trembling, his bow slipping, his mind overwhelmed. The entire scripture is therefore a manual for transforming fear into courage. The path is not the suppression of fear but its transcendence through self-knowledge, dharma, and surrender to the Divine.
