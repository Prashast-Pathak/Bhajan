Topic Intro: The Bhagavad Gita begins with grief — Arjuna's collapse on the battlefield is described in heartbreaking detail. This is significant: the greatest spiritual text of Hinduism begins not with philosophy but with raw human pain. The entire Gita is an answer to grief, and its wisdom on sorrow is both profound and practical.
