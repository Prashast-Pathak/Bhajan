प्रेम और भक्ति

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: The highest teaching on love in Sanatana Dharma is Bhakti — devotion as the most direct path to the Divine. The Gita's final chapters reveal that love, when purified of possessiveness and expectation, becomes a path to liberation. Divine love — as exemplified by Radha-Krishna, Hanuman's devotion to Rama, and the Gopis' surrender — is the highest form of human experience.
