चिंता और व्याकुलता

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: The ancient wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita and Upanishads offers profound guidance on overcoming anxiety. Lord Krishna teaches that most anxiety arises from attachment to outcomes we cannot control. The scriptures reveal that the true Self — the Atman — is beyond all suffering, and resting in this knowledge is the greatest remedy for worry.
