क्रोध और आत्म-संयम

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Topic Intro: The Bhagavad Gita identifies anger as one of the three gateways to self-destruction, alongside desire and greed. Yet it does not condemn righteous anger — Arjuna's battle was an act of dharmic fury. The scriptures teach us to understand anger's source, redirect its energy, and cultivate the equanimity that ultimately transcends it.
