Bhagavad Gita 2.66

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: नास्ति बुद्धिरयुक्तस्य न चायुक्तस्य भावना। न चाभावयतः शान्तिरशान्तस्य कुतः सुखम्॥

Meaning: One who has no self-control has no wisdom, nor concentration. Without concentration there is no peace, and without peace how can there be happiness?
