Bhagavad Gita 2.55

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: प्रजहाति यदा कामान्सर्वान्पार्थ मनोगतान्। आत्मन्येवात्मना तुष्टः स्थितप्रज्ञस्तदोच्यते॥

Meaning: O Partha, when a man completely casts off all the desires of the mind, and is satisfied in the Self by the Self alone — he is said to be one of steady wisdom.
