Bhagavad Gita 2.45

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: त्रैगुण्यविषया वेदा निस्त्रैगुण्यो भवार्जुन। निर्द्वन्द्वो नित्यसत्त्वस्थो निर्योगक्षेम आत्मवान्॥

Meaning: The Vedas are concerned with the three modes of material nature, O Arjuna. Rise above these three modes. Be free from duality, ever situated in the mode of goodness, free from thoughts of gain and safety, and be established in the Self.
