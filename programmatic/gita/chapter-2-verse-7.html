Bhagavad Gita 2.7

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: कार्पण्यदोषोपहतस्वभावः पृच्छामि त्वां धर्मसम्मूढचेताः। यच्छ्रेयः स्यान्निश्चितं ब्रूहि तन्मे शिष्यस्तेऽहं शाधि मां त्वां प्रपन्नम्॥

Meaning: My nature has been overcome by the taint of cowardice. My mind is confused about what is right. I ask you what is decidedly good for me. I am your disciple — please instruct me. I have surrendered to you.
