Bhagavad Gita 2.62

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: ध्यायतो विषयान्पुंसः सङ्गस्तेषूपजायते। सङ्गात्सञ्जायते कामः कामात्क्रोधोऽभिजायते॥

Meaning: By contemplating sense objects, a person develops attachment to them. From attachment arises desire, and from desire comes anger.
