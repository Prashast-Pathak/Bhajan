Bhagavad Gita 2.72

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: एषा ब्राह्मी स्थितिः पार्थ नैनां प्राप्य विमुह्यति। स्थित्वास्यामन्तकालेऽपि ब्रह्मनिर्वाणमृच्छति॥

Meaning: This is the transcendental state, O Partha. Having attained it, one is never deluded. Established in this state even at the time of death, one attains liberation into Brahman.
