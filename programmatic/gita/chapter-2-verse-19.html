Bhagavad Gita 2.19

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: य एनं वेत्ति हन्तारं यश्चैनं मन्यते हतम्। उभौ तौ न विजानीतो नायं हन्ति न हन्यते॥

Meaning: He who thinks that this (soul) is a slayer, and he who thinks that this (soul) has been slain — both of them fail to perceive the truth. This soul neither slays nor is slain.
