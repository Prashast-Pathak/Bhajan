Bhagavad Gita 2.23

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: नैनं छिन्दन्ति शस्त्राणि नैनं दहति पावकः। न चैनं क्लेदयन्त्यापो न शोषयति मारुतः॥

Meaning: The soul can never be cut by any weapon, nor burned by fire, nor moistened by water, nor dried by the wind.
