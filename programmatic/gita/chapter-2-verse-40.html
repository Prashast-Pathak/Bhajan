Bhagavad Gita 2.40

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: नेहाभिक्रमनाशोऽस्ति प्रत्यवायो न विद्यते। स्वल्पमप्यस्य धर्मस्य त्रायते महतो भयात्॥

Meaning: In this path, there is no loss of effort, nor is there any adverse result. Even a little practice of this spiritual discipline protects one from great fear.
