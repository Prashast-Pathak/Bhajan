Bhagavad Gita 2.71

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: विहाय कामान्यः सर्वान्पुमांश्चरति निःस्पृहः। निर्ममो निरहङ्कारः स शान्तिमधिगच्छति॥

Meaning: That man attains peace who, abandoning all desires, moves about without longing, without the sense of 'mine,' and without ego.
