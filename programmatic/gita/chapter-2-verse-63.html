Bhagavad Gita 2.63

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: क्रोधाद्भवति सम्मोहः सम्मोहात्स्मृतिविभ्रमः। स्मृतिभ्रंशाद्बुद्धिनाशो बुद्धिनाशात्प्रणश्यति॥

Meaning: From anger comes delusion, from delusion bewilderment of memory, from bewilderment of memory destruction of intelligence, and from destruction of intelligence one falls down.
