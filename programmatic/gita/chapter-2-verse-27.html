Bhagavad Gita 2.27

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: जातस्य हि ध्रुवो मृत्युर्ध्रुवं जन्म मृतस्य च। तस्मादपरिहार्येऽर्थे न त्वं शोचितुमर्हसि॥

Meaning: For one who has been born, death is certain; and for one who has died, birth is certain. Therefore, you should not grieve about the inevitable.
