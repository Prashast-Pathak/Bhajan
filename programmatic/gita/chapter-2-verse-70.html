Bhagavad Gita 2.70

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: आपूर्यमाणमचलप्रतिष्ठं समुद्रमापः प्रविशन्ति यद्वत्। तद्वत्कामा यं प्रविशन्ति सर्वे स शान्तिमाप्नोति न कामकामी॥

Meaning: Just as rivers flow into the ocean, which is ever being filled but is undisturbed — so too the person who remains undisturbed as all desires flow into him attains peace, not one who strives to fulfill desires.
