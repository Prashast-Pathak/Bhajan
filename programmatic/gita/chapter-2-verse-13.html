Bhagavad Gita 2.13

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: देहिनोऽस्मिन्यथा देहे कौमारं यौवनं जरा। तथा देहान्तरप्राप्तिर्धीरस्तत्र न मुह्यति॥

Meaning: Just as the soul passes in this body from childhood to youth to old age, so similarly at the time of death the soul passes to another body. The wise are not deluded by this.
