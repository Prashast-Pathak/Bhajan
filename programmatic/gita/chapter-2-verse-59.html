Bhagavad Gita 2.59

Programmatic spiritual reference page

Sanskrit: विषया विनिवर्तन्ते निराहारस्य देहिनः। रसवर्जं रसोऽप्यस्य परं दृष्ट्वा निवर्तते॥

Meaning: Though the embodied soul may be restricted from sense enjoyment, the taste for sense objects remains. But ceasing even this taste, the higher taste is found by seeing the Supreme.
