🪔
Ruling Deity & Cosmic Role

The presiding deity of Magha Nakshatra is Pitrus (Ancestors). This divine force shapes the soul of every being born under this star, imparting their celestial qualities, cosmic duties, and spiritual gifts. By devotion to this deity through bhajan and mantra, native souls harmonise with their highest dharmic potential and unlock the blessings encoded in their birth star.
