Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

📅 Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

सनातन ज्ञान सागर ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website.

We are a free, non-commercial platform dedicated to sharing Hindu spiritual knowledge. We do not sell your data, ever.

2. Information We Collect

We collect very little data. Specifically:

  • Locally stored data only: Your saved/favorited bhajans are stored in your browser's localStorage — this data never leaves your device and we cannot access it.
  • Anonymous usage analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics) that do not use cookies and do not track individual users. These provide only aggregate page view counts.
  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us via the contact form, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message).

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our website uses no third-party tracking cookies from advertisers.

If we serve Google AdSense advertisements in the future, Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Tiro Devanagari Hindi, Lato). Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Cloudflare Pages — for website hosting. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies.
  • Google AdSense (future) — if enabled, Google's privacy and cookie policies apply.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages and is designed to be accessible to elderly users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Since we store only locally saved preferences (in your own browser), you can:

  • Clear your saved bhajans at any time from the Saved page
  • Clear all site data by clearing your browser's localStorage and cache
  • Contact us to request deletion of any contact form submissions

7. Data Security

This is a static website with no backend server, no database, and no user accounts. There is no server-side storage of personal data. Your saved content lives only on your own device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
