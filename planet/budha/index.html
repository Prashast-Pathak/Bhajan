Budha (Mercury)

बुध देव
Ruling Deity: Lord Ganesha / Vishnu 🗓 Auspicious Day: Wednesday
Why This Deity?

The Cosmic Connection

Lord Ganesha rules intellect, speech, and business, which are the exact domains of Mercury in Vedic Astrology.

The Sacred Mantra
Sanskrit
ॐ ब्रां ब्रीं ब्रौं सः बुधाय नमः
Hindi
ओम ब्रां ब्रीं ब्रौं सः बुधाय नमः
English Transliteration
Om Braam Breem Broum Sah Budhaya Namah
Recommended Bhajans

Sacred Songs for Budha (Mercury)

  • Ganesha Pancharatnam
  • Vakratunda Mahakaya
  • Vishnu Sahasranama
Astrological Guidance

🕐When to Chant

Chant on Wednesdays, during Budha Mahadasha, or for success in studies and business. Offer green grass (durva) and green fruits to Lord Ganesha. Students preparing for exams and professionals in communication, finance, or trade benefit enormously from daily chanting of this mantra to sharpen Mercury's analytical and communicative gifts.
